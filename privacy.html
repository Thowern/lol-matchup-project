Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect only publicly available League of Legends match data through the Riot Games API. No personal information is collected.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively for statistical analysis and educational research.

3. Data Storage

Match data is stored in Parquet format and processed locally. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

4. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

5. Third-Party Services

This website uses the Riot Games API. Please refer to Riot Games' privacy policy for their data practices.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us through the GitHub repository.
