Champion Pool Intelligence

Pool Builder Lab

Build a champion pool that truly covers the lane. After each selection, the system combines matchup coverage, the tail of the worst weaknesses, overall strength, damage variety, and reliability, then ranks the champions that add the most real value.

Pool evaluation, not player evaluation: the score measures the statistical completeness of the selections. It does not know your mastery, learning time, or personal preferences.
Available lanes—Roles available in the dataset.

Guiding principle: the builder does not look only for strong champions. It rewards the marginal value of the new pick, reduces the impact of small samples, and preserves weight for the worst matchups.
